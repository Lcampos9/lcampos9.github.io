Audience

Our intended audience is for everyone who wants to consult the weather in their respective location.

Age Group

It can basically be any person with any age. Weather consultation is not limited to a certain age group. I would say young people search more for weather information.

Ocupation

Not required

Education

Any person with the ability to read and that can use a PC or smartphone.

Personas

Luis Daniel Campos

Luis is at the university, he is 20 years old, he likes to play football and desires a nice weather everytime he practices

Sofia Campos

She works at a travel agency for her country, she is 25 years old, she likes to know different places and is always interested in checking the weather so she can plan her next trip.

Scenarios

What will be the weather today?, When is it gonna be sunny this week?, what are the chances to get snow next week, how wil be the weather for tomorrow?
